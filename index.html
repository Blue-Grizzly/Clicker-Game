Drone Strike

Destroy the APCs before the time runs out.

Destroying 1 APC grants 1 point.

But be careful not to hit the PeaceKeepers or the Red Cross.

Clicking the Peace Keeper or the Red Cross will lose you 1 HP.

Failing to click an enemy before it passes through will also cost 1 HP.

You need to survive with at least 15 points to win.

Good luck!
